    • Thank you for visiting my web portfolio! Here is a little about me: I graduated from Arizona State University in December 2017 with a degree in Business Marketing and Political Science. While pursuing my degree I created my own social networking website and mobile app called ASU CHAT that was my primary source of income for several years. I taught myself how to build the website with HTML/CSS and utilized Social Engine's robust PHP platform to manage the back-end functionality.

    Although this startup wasn't the success I hoped, starting a web business taught me many professional design skills and I started my own freelance Web Design business in 2014. I was able to put myself through college through Web Design and garnered a sincere respect for Web Development all through-out. After graduating from Arizona State University I found myself drawn to digital marketing and web design positions. While I was confident in my design and marketing skills; I knew my programming skills were limited.

    I felt that I belonged in the web industry and knew I had to develop my skillset if I was going to be competitive for the positions I wanted. I enrolled in the University of Arizona's full-time immersive 12 week Full Stack Web Development program and haven't looked back since.

    The programming bootcamp was very challenging and taught me to push myself past what I thought I was capable of. In a matter of months I became an HTML/CSS expert and even taught myself SASS/SCSS because I knew I was focused on doing front-end development. I developed a working expertise with frameworks such as Bootstrap and Materialize so I was able to understand any companies code if need be. I have worked with React, Angular and Handlebars so I understand the vast range of frameworks used to create websites in 2018.

    During the bootcamp I continued to revamp my creative design skills(Adobe) and began delving into the back-end. I learned best-practices when utilizing databases such as MySQL, Firebase and MongoDB. I am comfortable spinning up my own servers and prefer using Express to make the process easier. I thoroughly enjoy progamming and it has become an all-consuming passion of mine. Please feel free to contact me with any development inquiries.
